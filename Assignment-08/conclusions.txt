1. Local Beam Search

| Beam Width | Behavior                                     |
| ---------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| k = 3      | Faster but may get stuck in local optimum    |
| k = 5      | Better exploration                           |
| k = 10     | Higher chance of optimal solution but slower |


Conclusion

- Larger k improves solution quality
- Convergence depends on k
- Small k → fast but less accurate
- Large k → slower but more optimal


2. Genetic Algorithm

| Crossover | Behavior                  |
| --------- | ------------------------- |
| 1-Point   | Faster but less diversity |
| 2-Point   | Better diversity          |

Conclusion

- 2-point crossover improves exploration
- It usually converges faster to better solutions
